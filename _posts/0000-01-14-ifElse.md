### IF ... ELSE

Conditions are used with IF ... ELSE Statements
- Equals: a == b
- Not Equals: a != b
- Less than: a < b
- Less than or equal to: a <= b
- Greater than: a > b
- Greater than or equal to: a >= b

```if b > a:```<br/>
  ```print("b is greater than a")```
